## Interesting Ideas to Share (Beyond Coding)

### The Big Reveal: Spec-Driven Is Domain-Agnostic

- **The 4-file architecture works for ANY job**  
  - `constitution.md` → Company rules, brand voice, compliance, methodology  
  - `spec.md` → The specific goal (campaign, contract, hire, product)  
  - `plan.md` → Strategy and phases  
  - `tasks.md` → Executable checklist  
  - **Same structure. Different content. Every domain.**

- **7 proven Job Kits already emerging**:
  - **PM-Kit**: Lean Startup, RICE prioritization, user research → Product-Market Fit
  - **Marketing-Kit**: Brand voice, GDPR compliance, campaign goals → 20% lead increase
  - **Startup-Kit**: SAFE terms, Sequoia pitch deck, TAM/SAM → Seed round closed
  - **Edu-Kit**: Bloom's Taxonomy, active recall, accessibility → 70%+ course completion
  - **Legal-Kit**: Jurisdiction rules, risk tolerance, prohibited clauses → Zero legal issues
  - **Sales-Ops-Kit**: MEDDIC, forecasting rules, pipeline health → 90% forecast accuracy
  - **HR-Kit**: Hiring principles, interview rubrics, compensation bands → Top performer retained 2+ years
