# Agentii Secures $100M Series B to Scale AI for Finance

We're thrilled to announce that Agentii has closed a $100 million Series B funding round, led by leading venture capital firms with deep expertise in financial technology and enterprise AI.

## The Investment

The Series B round was led by **Andreessen Horowitz** with participation from **Sequoia Capital**, **General Catalyst**, and **Goldman Sachs Asset Management**. This brings Agentii's total funding to $150 million.

## What This Means

This funding will accelerate our mission to transform how financial institutions leverage AI for analysis and decision-making. Specifically, we'll be investing in:

### Product Development

- **Matrix 2.0 and Beyond**: Accelerating development of our next-generation analysis engine
- **Enterprise Features**: Building advanced features for large-scale institutional deployments
- **API Platform**: Expanding programmatic access to Agentii capabilities
- **Industry Solutions**: Developing specialized solutions for banking, insurance, and asset management

### Global Expansion

- **European Operations**: Opening offices in London and Frankfurt to serve European financial institutions
- **Asia-Pacific Presence**: Establishing operations in Singapore and Tokyo
- **Regional Support**: Building local teams to provide region-specific support and compliance expertise

### Team Growth

We're scaling our team across all functions:
- **Engineering**: Expanding our AI and platform engineering teams
- **Product**: Building out product management and design capabilities
- **Sales & Customer Success**: Growing our go-to-market organization
- **Research**: Expanding our AI research team to develop next-generation capabilities

## Market Traction

This funding comes on the heels of significant customer growth:

- **300% year-over-year revenue growth**
- **50+ enterprise customers** including top-tier investment banks and asset managers
- **$14 trillion in assets under management** represented by our customer base
- **99.5% customer retention rate**

## Investor Perspective

"Agentii is solving a fundamental problem in financial services: how to leverage AI at institutional scale with the transparency and reliability that financial institutions require," said **Martin Casado**, General Partner at Andreessen Horowitz. "Their approach to agentic AI and transparent processing is exactly what the industry needs."

"Financial institutions are hungry for AI solutions that actually work in production," added **Jess Lee**, Partner at Sequoia Capital. "Agentii has proven they can deliver enterprise-grade AI that financial professionals trust and rely on."
