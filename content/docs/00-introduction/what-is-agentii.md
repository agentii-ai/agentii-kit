# What is Agentii?

Agentii is an AI-powered research platform designed for institutional financial analysis. It accelerates financial analysis by enabling AI agents to simultaneously evaluate complex documents, perform multiple valuation methodologies, and synthesize insightsâ€”work that traditionally requires hours of manual research.

## How Agentii Fits Your Workflow

If you're familiar with Bloomberg Terminal or FactSet, think of Agentii as a complementary layer on top of those tools. Where traditional terminals excel at real-time market data and standardized metrics, Agentii excels at:

- **Multi-document synthesis**: Analyzing 10-Ks, earnings transcripts, research reports, and expert insights simultaneously
- **Concurrent analysis**: Running DCF, comparable company, and precedent transaction valuations in parallel
- **Rapid iteration**: Asking follow-up questions and refining analysis in real time
- **Methodology validation**: Cross-checking conclusions across different analytical approaches

### Example: Pre-Earnings Analysis

**Traditional workflow** (3-4 hours):
1. Review analyst consensus estimates manually
2. Calculate your own DCF model
3. Analyze historical earnings surprises
4. Synthesize findings into thesis

**Agentii workflow** (30 minutes):
1. Create a project with three agents: Retrieval (fetch consensus), Valuation (run DCF), Risk (evaluate surprise factors)
2. Agents work in parallel
3. Review results, cross-check conclusions, refine thesis
4. Spend time on judgment and decision-making, not data gathering

## Two Ways to Use Agentii

Agentii provides two complementary interfaces for different research styles:

### Intelligence Hub: Project-Based Analysis
**For structured research campaigns** (earnings studies, sector rotations, M&A screening)

- Create projects organizing analysis around stocks or sectors
- Structure analysis into phases (pre-earnings, post-earnings, quarterly updates)
- Configure agents with specific prompts and instructions
- Monitor task progress and manage multiple parallel analyses
- Export results for presentations, reports, and sharing

**When to use Hub**: Planning comprehensive multi-week analyses, managing team workflows, building institutional knowledge

### Agent Workbench: Real-Time Exploration
**For exploratory, conversational analysis** (quick stock research, thesis validation, competitive intelligence)

- Submit natural language questions
- Agents respond immediately with relevant findings
- Ask follow-up questions and explore tangents
- Share sessions with colleagues
- Export transcripts for records

**When to use Workbench**: Quick 5-minute stock checks, diving deeper into unexpected results, testing new ideas

## What Agentii Cannot Do

Agentii is powerful but not a substitute for judgment. Understanding its limitations is essential:

- **No predictions**: Agents analyze historical and current information; they don't predict stock prices or predict future events
- **No portfolio management**: Agentii provides analysis; you make portfolio decisions
- **No replacement for compliance**: All agent output requires human review before use in client recommendations or investor decisions
- **No proprietary algorithms**: Agents use standard financial methodologies (DCF, comps, etc.); they don't employ secret strategies

## Key Use Cases

Agentii accelerates analysis across multiple financial domains:

### Equity Research
- **Initiating coverage** on new stocks: Multi-dimensional analysis in hours, not weeks
- **Earnings analysis**: Compare analyst consensus, historical patterns, and DCF scenarios simultaneously
- **Competitive analysis**: Analyze peer companies and their positioning at scale
- **Thesis monitoring**: Regular surveillance of key risk factors across your coverage

### Credit Analysis
- **Rating implications**: Analyze covenant adherence, leverage trends, credit metrics
- **Refinancing risk**: Evaluate maturity ladder, market conditions, refinancing capacity
- **Sector trends**: Monitor industry dynamics affecting credit profiles

### Merger & Acquisition
- **Target screening**: Rapid multi-dimensional assessment of acquisition candidates
- **Valuation analysis**: Compare multiple valuation approaches to establish pricing range
- **Integration planning**: Analyze synergy opportunities and risks

### Portfolio Management
- **Risk factor analysis**: Systematic evaluation of portfolio exposures to identified risks
- **Attribution analysis**: Understand which factors drove performance
- **Rebalancing**: Data-driven recommendations based on thesis changes

## Who Uses Agentii

Agentii serves different roles in financial institutions:

- **Equity research analysts**: Accelerate research velocity without compromising depth
- **Portfolio managers**: Validate investment theses and monitor risks efficiently
- **Credit analysts**: Evaluate credit metrics and refinancing scenarios
- **Investment bankers**: Screen targets, model valuations, assess strategic fit
- **Risk managers**: Monitor portfolio exposures and scenario test
- **Compliance teams**: Maintain documentation and audit trails

## Expected Learning Curve

Agentii is designed for financial professionals, so learning is quick:

- **15 minutes**: Create your first project, see agents in action
- **1 hour**: Complete first analysis end-to-end (Workbench query or Hub task)
- **1 day**: Comfortable with both Hub and Workbench interfaces
- **1 week**: Proficient with agent configuration, multi-agent orchestration
- **4 weeks**: Expert user, optimizing workflows, building team practices

No technical background required. If you're comfortable with Bloomberg Terminal or FactSet, you'll be productive in Agentii quickly.
